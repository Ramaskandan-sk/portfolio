# Images Folder

Place your images here:

## Required Images:

### 1. **logo.png** - Navbar logo
- Recommended size: 200x200px or larger (square)
- Format: PNG (with transparent background preferred)
- Will be displayed at 45x45px in navbar
- Should be your personal logo or initials design

### 2. **profile.jpeg** - Profile photo
- Recommended size: 500x500px or larger (square)
- Format: JPEG
- Professional headshot or portrait
- Good lighting and clear background
- Will be displayed as circular image on About page

## Tips for Logo:
- Use a square aspect ratio
- Transparent background works best
- Simple, recognizable design
- High contrast colors
- Can be your initials, icon, or brand mark

## Tips for Profile Photo:
- High-quality image
- Square aspect ratio
- Professional appearance
- Good lighting
- Clear background

Both images will have rounded corners and hover effects applied via CSS.
